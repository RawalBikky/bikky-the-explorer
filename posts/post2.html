5 Must-Visit Destinations from My Journey

1. Chikmagalur, Karnataka

A haven for bikers and coffee lovers. Winding roads, misty mornings, and lush hills made this ride unforgettable.

2. Gokarna Beach

Peaceful coastal town with serene beaches, perfect for a solo trip to clear your mind and soak in nature.

3. Bajhang, Nepal

My hometown, rich in heritage and simplicity. The mountains and raw landscapes tell stories of untouched beauty.

4. Coorg, Karnataka

Rode through fog-filled valleys and jungle trails. Known as the Scotland of India — and it truly lives up to it.

5. Hampi

Ancient ruins and dramatic stone landscapes. A historical marvel and biker’s dream destination in South India.
